Beta readiness checklist

Before DocCraftTools is shared publicly, every core page should meet our quality bar for trust, speed, clarity, accessibility and usefulness.

What must be true before beta?

  • Fast: pages should feel instant and avoid unnecessary scripts.
  • Accessible: users can navigate with keyboard and clear focus states.
  • Useful: every tool helps users complete a real business task.
  • Consistent: all workspaces follow the DocCraft design language.
  • Trustworthy: privacy, terms and contact pages remain visible and clear.
