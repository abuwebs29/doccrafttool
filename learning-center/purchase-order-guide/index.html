Purchase Order Basics

A purchase order documents what a business wants to buy from a supplier before delivery or invoicing.

Create Purchase OrderCreate Delivery Note

Why purchase orders matter

Purchase orders help businesses control spending, confirm supplier expectations and create a record before goods are delivered.

What to include

Include supplier details, PO number, order date, requested delivery date, item descriptions, quantities, prices and authorized contact.

Recommended workflow

  1. Create a purchase order.
  2. Send it to the supplier.
  3. Confirm delivery with a delivery note.
  4. Match the supplier invoice to the purchase order.
