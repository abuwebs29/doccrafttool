Testing script
Use this with every beta tester
Do not explain the product first. Give the tester a task and observe where they slow down.
  1. Create an invoice for a client.
    Measure whether they can find the tool and complete the document without help.
  2. Find a finance calculator.
    Ask them to calculate VAT or profit margin and watch the navigation path.
  3. Save one feedback note.
    Record the hesitation, confusing label or missing feature immediately.
