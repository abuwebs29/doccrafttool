Create professional rent receipts for tenants and landlords

A rent receipt confirms that a tenant has paid rent for a specific property and rental period. It provides proof of payment and helps both landlords and tenants keep accurate financial records.

Accurate rent receipts are useful for accounting, reimbursement requests, housing documentation, visa applications and dispute prevention. A completed rent receipt makes it clear when rent was paid, how much was paid and which period was covered.

What should a rent receipt include?

Include tenant name, landlord name, property address, receipt number, payment date, rental period, amount paid, payment method and any balance or notes.

Frequently asked questions

Is the rent receipt generator free?

Yes. Landlords, tenants and property managers can create rent receipts without an account.

What should a rent receipt include?

A rent receipt should include the payer, receiver, property address, rental period, payment date, amount and method.

Can I use it for monthly rent records?

Yes. Add the rental period and keep downloaded or printed copies for your records.
