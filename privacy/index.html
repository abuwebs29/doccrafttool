Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

DocCraftTools respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any information you provide while using this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

Most tools on DocCraftTools can be used without creating an account or providing personal information. Information entered into document generators is processed within your browser and is generally not submitted to our servers.

We may collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, referral sources, and general usage statistics. This information helps us improve the website, content, performance, and user experience.

Analytics

DocCraftTools may use analytics services such as Google Analytics to measure website traffic and understand user behavior. Analytics providers may use cookies or similar technologies to collect aggregated usage information.

Advertising

DocCraftTools may display advertisements through advertising partners, including Google AdSense. Third-party advertising vendors may use cookies to display ads based on a user's previous visits to this website or other websites.

Google may use advertising cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on user interests and browsing behavior. Users can manage ad personalization preferences through Google's Ad Settings.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on a device that help websites remember preferences and improve functionality. Cookies may be used for website functionality, analytics, performance measurement, advertising, personalization, and preference management.

Most browsers allow users to control or disable cookies through browser settings.

Third-Party Services

The website may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, hosting, security, and performance optimization. These providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Analytics
  • Google AdSense
  • Cloudflare
  • Website hosting providers

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the website and maintain the security of our services. However, no method of data transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

External Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

Children's Privacy

DocCraftTools is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or business practices. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact
