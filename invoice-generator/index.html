Create a professional invoice without complicated software

An invoice is a business document used to request payment from a customer or client. It explains who is billing, who is being billed, what products or services were provided, how much is owed, and when payment is due.

This invoice generator is designed for freelancers, consultants, contractors, agencies, service providers, and small businesses that need a simple way to create professional invoices. Instead of editing a spreadsheet manually, you can enter the details once and review the invoice instantly.

What should an invoice include?

A complete invoice should include business details, client details, invoice number, issue date, due date, itemized products or services, quantities, rates, tax, discounts, total amount, and payment instructions. Complete invoices look more professional and reduce payment delays.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Vague item descriptions
  • Missing due dates
  • Duplicate invoice numbers
  • Incorrect totals or tax details
  • Unclear payment instructions

Review your invoice before sending it to make sure all names, dates, amounts and terms are correct.
